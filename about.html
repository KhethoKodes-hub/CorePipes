Built from the Ground Up

FROM A SINGLE
TRADE COUNTER

Core Pipes & Fittings began as a family-run trade counter in Westmead, Durban — serving local plumbers with a handful of PVC pipe sizes and a commitment to honest pricing.

Our founders understood that contractors needed more than stock — they needed a partner who knew the material. Within five years, we expanded into full B2B wholesale distribution, supplying civil contractors, municipalities, and national retailers.

Today, from our 8,000 m² distribution centre, we move over 120 tonnes of PVC product monthly — and we still answer the phone ourselves.

What We Stand For

PRINCIPLES THAT
DRIVE US

Uncompromising Quality

Every product is tested to SANS 966 and ASTM D1785 standards before it leaves our floor. We do not stock sub-grade material.

Trade Partnerships

Bulk pricing, credit terms, and priority stock allocation for registered trade accounts. Every contractor is a partner.

Speed to Site

Same-day dispatch for orders before 12:00. 14 vehicles cover KZN daily with national courier for outlying regions.

Responsible Sourcing

PVC compound from certified local manufacturers. Offcuts processed through our in-house recycling programme.

Expert Advice

Our team includes qualified plumbers and civil engineers ready to spec the right product for any system.

Community Roots

Artisan plumbing bursaries annually and a free technical training programme for emerging contractors.
