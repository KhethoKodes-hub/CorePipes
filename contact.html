Common Questions

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

How long does it take to open a trade account?
Verified applications are typically approved within 1 business day. You'll need your CIPC registration, a copy of your ID, and a bank confirmation letter.
What is your minimum order quantity?
MOQs vary by product. Standard fittings have no minimum. Pipe lengths typically start at 20 units per size for wholesale pricing.
Do you deliver outside KwaZulu-Natal?
Yes. We deliver nationally through our Cape Town and Gauteng hubs, and use national courier partners.
Can I get custom pipe lengths cut to spec?
Absolutely. Our custom cutting service is available to all trade account holders.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept EFT, credit card, and account payments (for approved credit accounts).
What is your returns policy?
Unused, unopened stock can be returned within 30 days of delivery.
