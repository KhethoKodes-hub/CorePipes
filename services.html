FAQs
Common Questions

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

How long does it take to open a trade account?
Verified applications are typically approved within 1 business day. You'll need your CIPC registration, a copy of your ID, and a bank confirmation letter. Credit accounts may take 2–3 days pending credit checks.
What is your minimum order quantity?
MOQs vary by product. Standard fittings have no minimum. Pipe lengths typically start at 20 units per size for wholesale pricing. Full-pallet orders attract the best rates. Contact us for a project-specific BOM.
Do you deliver outside KwaZulu-Natal?
Yes. We deliver nationally through our Cape Town and Gauteng hubs, and use national courier partners for fittings and sundries. Delivery lead times vary by region — typically 1–3 business days outside metro areas.
Can I get custom pipe lengths cut to spec?
Absolutely. Our custom cutting service is available to all trade account holders. Submit a cut list via email or through the portal. Orders placed before 10:00 are cut and dispatched the same day.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept EFT, credit card, and account payments (for approved credit accounts). COD is available for standard orders. All invoices are issued immediately on dispatch with 30/60-day terms for qualifying accounts.
What is your returns policy?
Unused, unopened stock can be returned within 30 days of delivery. We arrange collection at no charge for orders over R2,500. Credit notes are processed within 48 hours. Defective stock is handled under manufacturer warranty.
